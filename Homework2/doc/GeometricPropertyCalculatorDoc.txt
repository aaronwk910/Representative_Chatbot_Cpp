This program takes as input a type of shape (rectangle, triangle, or circle) as well as up to 3 numbers.
The numbers denote the length and width of the rectangle, the radius of the circle, or the three side lengths
of the triangle. Extraneous numbers are ignored.

Sample input is as follows, with a command line argument of 1

circle 7 3 4

sample output is as follows. The number 7 denotes the radius of the circle. 3 and 4 are ignored.

CIRCLE AREA 153.938040
